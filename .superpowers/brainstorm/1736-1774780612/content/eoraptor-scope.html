Eoraptor Prototype Scope

Full pixel-sprite prototype for species 001 across hatchling, juvenile, and adult using a two-pass AI workflow.

Pipeline
1. Anchor frame approval
Pick one clean Eoraptor frame per stage that defines silhouette, palette, and camera.
2. Pose exploration pass
Generate rough mood poses and shortlist the best idle / happy / sleepy / sick motion ideas.
3. Final strip pass
Regenerate each chosen loop as a full strip with stronger consistency and cleaner timing.
4. Normalize + wire
Crop, align, scale, and export game-ready sheets for the Eoraptor stages only.
